# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

## QA / Testing Engineers

### Role Summary
QA Engineers validate quality, test features against acceptance criteria, and ensure the product meets standards before release. They work closely with Developers and Product Managers to prevent defects and verify functionality.

### Responsibilities
- Create and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Validate acceptance criteria are met
- Participate in release readiness reviews

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain test coverage and documentation

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups with development team
- Bug triage meetings
- Test plan reviews with Product and Engineering
- Release readiness sign-off

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach teams on agile best practices. They enable the team to self-organize and continuously improve their processes.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Shield the team from external disruptions
- Foster collaboration between Product, Engineering, and Stakeholders
- Track and improve team velocity and process metrics

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Maintain healthy agile practices
- Build a culture of continuous improvement
- Ensure transparency and effective communication

### Typical Communication
- Daily standup facilitation
- Sprint ceremonies (planning, review, retrospective)
- One-on-one coaching with team members
- Coordination with Project Manager on dependencies and risks

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Manager:** Collaborates on process improvements, risk management, and dependency tracking
- **Developers:** Supports in daily standups, removes blockers, coaches on agile practices
- **Product Manager:** Facilitates backlog refinement and sprint planning sessions

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create and validate user experiences, workflows, and interface designs. They ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs through research, prototyping, and usability testing.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and create personas
- Design wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Validate designs through usability testing
- Collaborate with Product on feature specifications
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Maintain design systems and component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Validate designs with real users before development
- Ensure consistency across the product
- Balance user needs with technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product and Engineering
- User research findings presentations
- Usability testing sessions
- Handoff meetings with Developers

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager:** Collaborates on feature specs, user flows, and acceptance criteria
- **Developers:** Works closely on design implementation and technical feasibility
- **Business Analyst:** Aligns user experience with business requirements

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, automate deployments, and manage infrastructure. They enable rapid, reliable releases and maintain operational excellence.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Automate build, test, and deployment processes
- Manage infrastructure as code
- Monitor system health and performance
- Implement security and compliance controls in pipelines
- Coordinate deployment schedules and release windows

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize deployment failures and downtime
- Improve developer productivity through automation
- Maintain infrastructure reliability and scalability

### Typical Communication
- Deployment planning with Project Manager
- Pipeline integration with Developers and QA
- Infrastructure reviews with Security Officer
- Incident response and post-mortems

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Integrates code into CI/CD pipelines, provides feedback on build issues
- **QA:** Coordinates automated testing in pipelines and staging environments
- **Project Manager:** Aligns on deployment schedules and release readiness
- **Security/Compliance Officer:** Implements security scanning and compliance checks

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. They analyze business needs, document requirements, and ensure solutions align with organizational goals.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create detailed functional specifications
- Validate that solutions meet business needs
- Provide data analysis and insights to inform decisions
- Facilitate workshops and requirements gathering sessions

### Goals
- Ensure solution-business alignment
- Translate business needs into actionable requirements
- Reduce rework through clear, validated requirements
- Enable data-driven decision making

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with Stakeholders
- Specification reviews with Product and Engineering
- Data analysis presentations
- User acceptance testing coordination

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager:** Provides data and analysis to inform prioritization decisions
- **Project Manager:** Clarifies requirements and validates scope
- **UX Designer:** Ensures user experience aligns with business requirements
- **Developers:** Translates business requirements into technical specifications

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads represent the customer support organization, channeling customer feedback into product improvements and ensuring smooth post-release support. They close the loop between customers and the product team.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and escalate customer-reported issues
- Share customer feedback and pain points with Product and Engineering
- Coordinate knowledge transfer for new releases
- Ensure support documentation is current
- Track and analyze support metrics and trends
- Participate in release planning and readiness reviews

### Goals
- Minimize customer friction and support escalations
- Ensure timely resolution of customer issues
- Improve product based on customer feedback
- Enable support team effectiveness through documentation and training

### Typical Communication
- Weekly feedback sessions with Product Manager
- Release readiness reviews with PM and QA
- Post-release triage meetings
- Customer escalation coordination

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager:** Shares customer insights and feature requests
- **QA:** Coordinates on bug verification and regression testing
- **Project Manager:** Participates in release planning and communication
- **Developers:** Escalates critical customer issues for investigation

## Security / Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Security and Compliance Officers enforce security policies, audit compliance, and ensure the product meets regulatory and security standards. They proactively identify risks and guide teams on security best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs and code for security vulnerabilities
- Enforce security and privacy policies
- Conduct security audits and compliance reviews
- Manage security incident response
- Provide security training and guidance
- Maintain security documentation and controls

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities and data breaches
- Ensure regulatory compliance (GDPR, SOC2, etc.)
- Build security awareness across teams
- Minimize security-related risks and incidents

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews with Engineering
- Compliance audit reports to leadership
- Incident response coordination
- Policy updates and training sessions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager:** Consults on security requirements and privacy considerations
- **Developers:** Reviews code for security vulnerabilities, provides secure coding guidance
- **DevOps Engineer:** Implements security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
- **Project Manager:** Escalates security risks and compliance blockers
